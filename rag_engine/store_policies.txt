# STORE POLICIES AND GUIDELINES

## Return Policy
We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all unopened and unused products. For electronics (laptops, phones), the return window is 14 days, and a 10% restocking fee applies if the seal is broken. Clothing can be returned within 30 days as long as the original tags are attached. 

## Shipping & Delivery
Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days. Expedited shipping takes 1-2 business days and costs an additional $15. Free standard shipping applies automatically to all orders over $50. Orders placed after 2 PM EST will be processed the next business day.

## Warranties
All electronics come with a standard 1-year manufacturer warranty covering internal defects. Accidental damage (like dropping your phone) is not covered unless you purchased the Extended Protection Plan at checkout. 

## Budget and Payments
We support all major credit cards, PayPal, and Apple Pay. If a purchase exceeds your local budget limits set on our system, the transaction will issue a warning but will still process unless your credit card company declines it.

## Price Matching
We will match prices from major competitors (like Mockzon or Targmock) if you find a lower price on the identical item within 7 days of purchase. Just contact our support team.
